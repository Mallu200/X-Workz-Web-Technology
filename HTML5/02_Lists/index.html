How to Make Perfect Pasta

Ingredients You Will Need

  • 500g Spaghetti
  • Fresh Tomatoes
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Fresh Basil Leaves
  • Parmesan Cheese

Cooking Instructions

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water.
  2. Add the pasta and cook for 9 minutes until "al dente."
  3. While pasta cooks, saut√© garlic in olive oil.
  4. Toss the pasta with the oil and fresh tomatoes.
  5. Garnish with basil and cheese.

Kitchen Vocabulary

Al dente
Pasta that is cooked to bbe firm to the bite.
Saute
To fry quickly in a little hot fat or oil.
